Dependencies:

Underscore.js – hard dependency, needs to be included (http://underscorejs.org/)

jQuery.js – for RESTful persistence, history support with Backbone.Router and DOM manipulation with Backbone.View (http://jquery.com/)























Method 1:

❅ Open the link to the official website: http://backbonejs.org

❅ Scroll down to Downloads and Dependencies

❅ Right click on the desired version and click 'Save As'

❅ Development version: full source Javascript library ❅ Production version: Backbone-min.js library ❅ Edge version: in development




Method 2:

❅ External file hosting

❅ The CDNs for all of the libraries (dependencies included):
